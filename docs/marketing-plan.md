# Stamply Marketing Plan (Prototype Phase)

## Positioning

**Stamply** turns travel memories into collectible AI passport stamps.

## ICP (initial)

- Frequent travelers (20–40)
- City-break travelers and digital nomads
- Social sharers who post travel content

## Core Message

"Unlock cities. Collect beautiful stamps. Build your travel passport."

## Launch Assets (Prototype)

1. 30-second app loop demo video
2. 5 mock social posts (before/after travel story)
3. Landing page with waitlist CTA
4. Product Hunt prep copy

## Growth Experiments (2 weeks)

1. TikTok/IG Reels: “I turned my trips into collectible city stamps”
2. Referral waitlist: unlock exclusive stamp style preset
3. Creator seeding: 10 travel creators get early build

## Metrics to Track

- Stamp unlock rate
- Passport completion rate (1st week)
- Share-to-feed rate
- D1 and D7 retention (prototype cohorts)

## Next Steps

- Capture real UI footage from this prototype
- Build a simple waitlist page
- Start content testing with 3 hooks and 3 creatives
